About me

I am Joe Morel, a telematics engineer and web developer, graduated from Pontificia Universidad Madre y Maestra (PUCMM). I have extensive experience in JavaScript, TypeScript, React.js, Next.js, PHP, HTML/CSS, and Tailwind CSS. I am proficient in design tools such as Figma, Adobe Illustrator, and Photoshop, and skilled in integrating API's and using version control systems like Git.

I also have backend experience with Node.js and Google Firebase, and work with databases like MySQL and Microsoft SQL Server. I manage web projects using platforms like Asana and Trello, and deploy on Vercel, Netlify, and WordPress/Cpanel.
