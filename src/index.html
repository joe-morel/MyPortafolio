About me

My name is Joe Morel, and I am a telematics engineer graduate from the Pontificia Universidad Madre y Maestra (PUCMM). I have had a passion for art and technology from an early age, and this has fueled my interest in combining the two. As a result, I am a graphic designer and web developer, bringing my love for design and technology to each project I work on.

I have experience with a variety of tools, including Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, AutoCAD, Sketch, Figma, Microsoft Office, as well as various programming languages, from C and Java to web programming with JavaScript and React.js. I am also skilled at incorporating APIs into my projects and am comfortable working with operating systems like Windows, Linux, and Mac, with Mac being my primary environment.

In addition to my technical skills, I have experience directing and managing telecommunications projects focused on optical fibers. My ability to lead and work in teams makes me a highly valued professional in the industry.

With my broad knowledge and experience in web design and development, I am always seeking ways to innovate and improve my work, delivering creative and effective solutions for my clients.
